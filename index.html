About Us

Our Mission

The mission of Watershed Recovery is to provide an expert and focused guidance on achieving a sustainable watershed planning and management while considering technical, socio-economical and environmental criteria.

Our Vision

Watershed recovery takes a systems thinking approach to sustainably managing watershed, plants and people.

Founder

Prof. Joe Magner is the founder of Watershed Recovery. He started this consultancy in the year 2005 to use his expertise in watershed management for the betterment of the society. Currently, he is a Research Professor in University of Minnesota since 2011. He also served as a Principal Research Scientist, MN Pollution Control Agency (MPCA), Impaired Waters Nonpoint Source Management Program from 2006-2013. For more information click below.
